## 5. Customer Problem

- 쉐이크를 매번 직접 흔들어야 한다.

- 덩어리가 남는다.
- 세척이 귀찮다.
- 가방에 넣으면 샐까 봐 걱정된다.
- 일반 쉐이커는 생활감이 강하고 예쁘지 않다.

## 14. Generation Workflow

Preferred workflow:

1. Upload product-specific assets under product folder.
2. AI classifies assets.
3. Select hero image, lifestyle image, mixing image, cleaning image, mobility image.
4. Build card-specific prompts.
5. Generate card backgrounds or compositions.
6. Add final Korean text in Figma.
7. QA product accuracy.
8. Save result to Project Archive.

---

## 15. Batch Generation Rule

To generate 10 cards faster, do not request one collage image.

Instead, generate a card pack using one master style and ten separate card prompts.

Rules:

- Each card must be exported as a separate 1080x1350 image.
- Keep the same product reference.
- Keep the same lighting and color system.
- Do not place all 10 cards inside one image.
- Text-heavy parts should be completed in Figma.

---

## 16. QA Checklist

Before publishing, check:

- Is the product shape correct?
- Is the logo correct?
- Is the product color correct?
- Is the transparent material preserved?
- Are specs correct?
- Is Korean text readable on mobile?
- Is each card one message only?
- Does the full carousel flow naturally?
- Does it feel like Lifestyle Editorial, not cheap e-commerce?
